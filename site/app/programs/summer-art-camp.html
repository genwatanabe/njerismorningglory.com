This program is designed for 3-15 year olds and will be held from 9:00 am to 1:00 pm, Monday through Friday. We encourage the children to try a variety of different art mediums. We place great emphasis on the process of making art, not just the final product itself.

{{slide.text}}

 

Some of the art activities we offer are:

  • Clay projects involving sculpturing, the use of a potter’s wheel, slab work, extruding and paper clay.
  • Textile activities includes fabric painting, hand sewing and machine sewing.
  • Weaving with a loom to create table runners and vests.
  • Cooking activities such as baking and making preserves and jam.
  • Glass Fusion to create decorative objects of art.

To register for this art class, please complete the registration form to secure your place in this summer’s program. The forms must be completed and returned to us by May 2nd. Once we receive your registration, you will receive confirmation of your enrollment
