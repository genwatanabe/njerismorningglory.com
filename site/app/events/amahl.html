Adapted from the opera by Gian Carlo Menotti,

Amahl the shepherd lives with his mother. Though they are poor and he is crippled, Amahl is a happy child with a fondness for tall tales. One clear night while playing his flute, Amahl sees a star with a tail, like none he has seen before. Three strangers follow the star to Amahl's home carrying gifts for a miracle child, not knowing about the miracle this night brings...

Bring children, family, and friends!
