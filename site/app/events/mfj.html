After dark, the night aglow,
Into the forest the children will go!

THE MAGIC FOREST JOURNEY is a night time lantern walk through our beautiful school grounds. Children are guided by a singing angel to find fully costumed fairytales coming to life along a Jack-o-lantern trail.

Children receive a gift from each fairytale.

Though this event arrives near Halloween, THE MAGIC FOREST JOURNEY is not a scary experience.

After the moon rises, the gentle night is illuminated with magic and filled with the drama of many classical tales. Dancing forest fairies and the pointy hats of elves will be sighted, and the sound of flutes floating upon the breeze will be heard.

Join us for this unforgettable family experience.

The Magic Forest Journey is held in late October.
