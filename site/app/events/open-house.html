We are seeking students for all programs and grades—preschool/ kindergarten (ages 2 to 5 years old) through 5th grade—and have openings now. However; this opportunity is for parents only to come understand our grades programs – kindergarten and grades 1 to 5.

Enjoy a tour of our amazing school, engage in a hands-on Waldorf experience, meet our teachers, and learn about our curriculum and what we have to offer.

"Our school offers the broader community an opportunity to educate their children for the 21st century and into the future in an environment that is highly holistic, thoughtful, and engaging," says Njeri McGillicuddy, Waldorf teacher, master artist, and founder.
